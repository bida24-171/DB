Deja Brew is a family business that was founded by Leo James in 1960 and has been run by his descendants ever since.
Leo was working as a farmer and learned a lot about coffee beans as it was one of the crops being grown.
He would experiment with the beans and other ingredients during his breaks and when he was on leave until he
made a cup of coffee that he had never tasted his entire life. He would give his co-workers a sample during the breaks and get their reviews.
A woman he worked with gave him some tips on how he should start his own business but ended up running it together.
As time went on by, they got married using their life earnings from the coffee shop.
They had children who helped them keep their shop with loyal customers.
Deja Brew is currently open in the town of Mystic Falls and is still under the governance of the grandkids.
They are still on the aim to help their grandfather achieve his dream of blessing the world with his creation.
